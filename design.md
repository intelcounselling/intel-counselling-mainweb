🌿 Website Theme: Serene Premium Wellness
Overall Mood
The site strikes a balance between clinical authority and warm human empathy — it feels premium and trustworthy without being cold or corporate. The tagline "Healing Begins with Understanding" perfectly encapsulates this duality.

Color Palette
Role	Color	Usage
Background	#F4EFE6 — warm parchment/cream	Base background, gives an organic, calming feel
Primary Dark	#1A1A1A — near-black charcoal	Text, nav, CTA buttons
Serene Green	#1C3F39 — deep forest green	Brand accent, hero headline, interactive elements
Brass/Terracotta	#C19B6C — warm brass gold	Micro-accents, labels, icon highlights
Section Dark	#2A2825 / zinc-900	Feature/service card backgrounds (dark contrast sections)
The palette is earthy, natural, and premium — evoking nature, calm, and trust.

Typography
Headings: Playfair Display (serif, weight 900) — editorial, elegant, gives the brand gravitas
Body: Outfit (sans-serif, light/medium) — clean, modern, readable
Together they create a classic-meets-modern editorial aesthetic
Visual Design Language
Glassmorphism — frosted glass cards with backdrop-blur on floating badges
Soft ambient blobs — large blurred radial gradients (blur-[90px]) that shift with mouse movement, creating a living, breathing background
Stacking scroll sections — full-height sticky sections that transition as you scroll
Spotlight cards — interactive glow effect that follows the cursor across feature and service cards
3D depth — hero image uses translateZ layering and preserve-3d to create a dimensional card effect
Grain/organic texture — overall warmth from cream base color prevents the dark sections from feeling harsh
Interaction & Animation
Magnetic buttons — CTA buttons subtly follow cursor movement
Split text animation — headings animate letter-by-letter on page load with editorial fade-up effect
Rotating text — services section cycles through words ("unique", "personal", "academic", "career") with a character-by-character spring animation
Parallax mouse tracking — hero elements shift in opposite directions as the mouse moves, adding depth
Micro-animations — floating trust badge, pulsing shield icon, heartbeat animations
Reveal-on-scroll — FadeIn components stagger content as sections enter the viewport
